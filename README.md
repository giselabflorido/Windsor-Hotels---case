# Case Study: Windsor Marapendi Opening & Large-Scale Hotel Operations Management

## Overview
This project presents my role in structuring the entire people operation for the opening of Windsor Marapendi, a large-scale hotel in Barra da Tijuca (Rio de Janeiro), as well as the subsequent management of a multi-property hospitality complex.

---

## Business Context

- Opening of a 5-star hotel focused on premium service and events  
- Highly people-dependent 24/7 operation  
- Need for rapid scalability  
- Preparation for major international events (Olympics, peak tourism demand)  

---

## Project Structure

### Windsor Marapendi
- 487 rooms  
- 44 event spaces  
- 24/7 operation (3 shifts)  

---

## Implementation Phases

### Phase 1 – Soft Opening (Oct/2015)
- ~150 rooms operational  
- ~150 employees  
- Initial operational validation  

### Phase 2 – Full Opening (Dec/2015 – New Year’s Eve)
- Expansion to full capacity  
- Fully operational structure  
- Prepared for high-demand period

## My Role

### Recruitment & Workforce Planning
- Led full-scale recruitment for the new hotel  
- Designed and executed headcount planning for full operation  
- Managed internal staff transfers across properties  

---

### Onboarding & Training
- Designed and implemented onboarding processes  
- Delivered operational and behavioral training programs  
- Prepared teams for high service standards  

---

### Quality & Compliance
- Prepared the operation for opening audit  
- Ensured compliance with international hospitality standards (Preferred Hotels)  
- Supported the hotel launch with operational excellence
